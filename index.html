Our Market

James’ Fabrics is a one stop shop for all your fabric needs. We have a wide variety of fabrics from around the world but we specialize in fabrics from Africa. We have many styles and textures to choose from and we can ship it anywhere in the world.

Stop by Mercy’s Clothes for the latest in trendy fassion. We have private dressing rooms and a tailor on staff for any of your alterations needs. So if there is a particular garment you like and it doesn’t quite fit, we can handle that within one week.

Visit Connie and her coconuts for a refreshing snack. On a warm shopping day, you can be certain that Connie has cool refreshments for you, or if you like, a wide variety of coffees and teas.

Have lunch at Fitzroy’s Foods. Shopping can work up and appetite and you need fuel to keep going. Specializing in African cuisine, Fitzroy also has dishes from various regions of the world and can customize a dish to your tastes.
